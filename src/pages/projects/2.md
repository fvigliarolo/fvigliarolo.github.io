<h2 data-locale-en="About this project" data-locale-es="Sobre este proyecto">About this project</h2>

<p data-locale-en="This system has two roles: student and teacher." data-locale-es="Este sistema tiene dos roles: alumno y profesor.">This system has two roles: student and teacher.</p>

<p data-locale-en="From a dashboard, teachers are responsible for creating daily boxing exercise routines. Meanwhile, students can view the routine that the teacher has created." data-locale-es="Desde un dashboard, los profesores crean rutinas diarias de boxeo. Meanwhile, students can view the routine that the teacher has created.</p>

<p data-locale-en="This was a simple project aimed at learning how to use backend and databases as services. <h2 data-locale-en="Walkthrough" data-locale-es="Recorrido">Walkthrough</h2>

<p data-locale-en="I started this project while looking for a solution to a daily problem I faced at my gym. During my boxing training sessions, there were too many students, which overwhelmed the coach and caused delays when I needed to ask about the day's workout routine." data-locale-es="Empecé este proyecto buscando una solución a un problema cotidiano que tenía en mi gimnasio. Durante los entrenamientos de boxeo había demasiados alumnos, eso sobrecargaba al entrenador y generaba demoras cada vez que necesitaba consultar la rutina del día.">I started this project while looking for a solution to a daily problem I faced at my gym. During my boxing training sessions, there were too many students, which overwhelmed the coach and caused delays when I needed to ask about the day's workout routine.</p>

<p data-locale-en="To solve this, I decided to develop a system where the coach could create the daily workout plan, allowing students to access the website and view the routine and training order for each day." data-locale-es="Para resolverlo, decidí desarrollar un sistema donde el entrenador pudiera cargar la rutina diaria y los alumnos pudieran entrar al sitio para verla junto con el orden de entrenamiento de cada jornada.">To solve this, I decided to develop a system where the coach could create the daily workout plan, allowing students to access the website and view the routine and training order for each day.</p>

<p data-locale-en="From a technical perspective, one of my goals was to avoid developing a custom backend. To achieve this, I researched existing solutions and discovered Backend as a Service (BaaS) platforms." data-locale-es="Desde lo técnico, uno de mis objetivos era evitar desarrollar un backend propio. Para eso investigué soluciones existentes y descubrí las plataformas Backend as a Service (BaaS).">From a technical perspective, one of my goals was to avoid developing a custom backend. To achieve this, I researched existing solutions and discovered Backend as a Service (BaaS) platforms.</p>

<p data-locale-en="After building the frontend for the application, I chose Supabase as the backend. Once the system was complete, I explored free deployment options." data-locale-es="Después de construir el frontend de la aplicación, elegí Supabase como backend. Una vez terminado el sistema, exploré opciones gratuitas para desplegarlo.">After building the frontend for the application, I chose Supabase as the backend. Once the system was complete, I explored free deployment options.</p>

<p data-locale-en="This led to my first deployment using Vercel and my first experience with Backend as a Service." data-locale-es="Eso me llevo a mi primer despliegue con Vercel y a mi primera experiencia real trabajando con Backend as a Service.">This led to my first deployment using Vercel and my first experience with Backend as a Service.</p>
